## 📝 Troubleshooting

If the trainer fails to draw the overlay:

1. Ensure the game runs in Windowed or Borderless Windowed mode. Fullscreen mode prevents the overlay from drawing over the game.
2. Check that your resolution settings match between the game and your desktop.
3. Verify that visual effects like overlays from Discord or Steam are not conflicting. Close those programs temporarily to test the connection.
4. Ensure you have the latest graphics driver installed for your GPU.

If the bot does not interact with the game properly:

1. Confirm that your keyboard layout is set to QWERTY.
2. Verify that your game resolution is native to your monitor.
3. Ensure the game is in the foreground when you start the bot process.
